# Claude Instagram Audit Research

## Summary

Instagram account audit work should become a structured analysis engine, not a one-off manual report.

The direction is:

```text
My Account -> Competitor Accounts -> Gap Analysis -> Recommended Actions
```

## Engines To Bring In

- Competitor Engine
- Audit Engine
- Brand DNA Engine
- Audience Engine
- Format Analyzer
- Hook Analyzer
- Content Pillar Engine
- Posting Time Analyzer
- Blind Spot Engine
- Growth Score Engine
- Content Score Engine

## KPI

- Save Rate
- Share Rate
- Comment Rate
- Hook Retention
- Brand Consistency

## Do Not Adopt Now

- Direct Windsor.ai integration

## AI-Content-OS Direction

Prioritize a lightweight account and competitor audit model that can guide card news topics, hooks, layouts, and publishing recommendations.
