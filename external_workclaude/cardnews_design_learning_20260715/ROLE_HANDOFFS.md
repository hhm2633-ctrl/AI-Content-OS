These are analysis handoffs, not implementation work orders. No lane may modify source screenshots, `modules/**`, `tests/**`, `storage/**`, shared project documents, WorkflowEngine, Git, publishing state or APIs under this learning RC.

## Team lead / integration

- Owns only `external_workclaude/cardnews_design_learning_20260715/**`.
- Keeps Source A/B as two post-level samples with ordered slide binding and crop/contamination metadata.
- Integrates only `benchmark_observed` facts and explicitly labels every transfer recommendation `hypothesis_only`.
- Does not register patterns in production, change code or treat this RC as design-performance validation.

## Copy · story

**Consume**

- A: number + material/use context + example/check + short tip.
- B: stage + when + result + primary content + takeaway.
- One slide/one claim; solution max three complete steps; one CTA.

**Do not consume**

- original prompts, captions, keyword phrase, exact hooks or near-copy;
- raw reactions as proof;
- “one AI solves everything,” absolute outcomes or unverified performance language.

**Future acceptance**

- four complete roles, no silent truncation, no unsupported claim, and reward CTA only when a real deliverable is verified.

## Layout · typography

**Consume**

- A as `notebook`-first skin tokens; handwritten accent only for title/short emphasis.
- B as `tutorial`-first repeat slots; max two content boxes.
- 1/4–4/4 progress and a separate CTA/attribution row.

**Do not consume**

- an eleventh layout, source progress totals, original material photos, handwriting, icons, doodles, exact palette or box composition.

**Future acceptance**

- 65px safe margin, ≥4.5:1 contrast, min 24px, and readable title/body/CTA/attribution at 1080 and 270px.

## Rendering · artifacts

**Consume**

- B repeatable skin/slots before A full material variation.
- Textless, independently approved background assets and separate Pillow/SVG text layers.
- A limited to one or two independent surface variants per four-card set with a flat-paper fallback.

**Do not consume**

- source pixels, Instagram UI, pseudo-Hangul, embedded text, watermark, copied prop layout or source iconography.

**Future acceptance**

- exact four PNG, decode/1080 square, canonical roles, valid glyph coverage, writable-rect containment, 270 visual QA and fallback preservation.

## Evidence · rights

**Consume**

- provenance, hash, ordered post/slide binding, crop bounds and contamination exclusions.
- Functional abstractions only: progression, hierarchy, slots and sequence.

**Do not consume**

- screenshots or copy as production assets, claim evidence, rights evidence or social proof;
- public counts as performance evidence;
- account/audio/comment data without a separate lawful, PII-scrubbed intake.

**Future acceptance**

- every production asset has owned/licensed/public-domain permission, topic relevance, reviewed time and attribution. These competitor files remain `render_allowed=false` permanently.

## Packaging · CTA

**Consume**

- progress as sequence metadata, not CTA copy;
- truthful next-slide cues;
- one primary action and optional one secondary action;
- reward availability/fulfillment fields only as a future contract proposal.

**Do not consume**

- assumed DM automation, unverified reward delivery, action stacking or source platform controls.

**Future acceptance**

- no keyword/DM CTA unless a real deliverable, operator, manual delivery mode, rights and compliance are verified. Package readiness never means actual publishing.

## Independent QA

**Required gates**

1. Exactly two posts and eleven observed slides; Source B 4/11 remains part of the same post.
2. Central frame crop only, with in-frame overlay masking and `manual_crop_required` on detection failure.
3. Public counts remain raw/not causal.
4. Rights remain competitor-reference/analysis-only/render-blocked.
5. Four roles and ten layouts remain immutable.
6. Vertical fit remains hypothesis-only, with regulated-category risk stated.
7. Body/CTA/attribution outrank decoration at 1080/270.

Any failure keeps the learning RC at NO-GO.
