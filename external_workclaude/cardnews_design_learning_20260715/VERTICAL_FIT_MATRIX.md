## Regulated and high-stakes topics

Medical, financial, legal and government-procedure content is not a standalone “fit” win. For these areas:

- official or expert current evidence is mandatory;
- attribution and caution space outrank decorative boxes;
- handwritten body copy and dense prompt panels are disfavored;
- Source A/B cannot serve as claim evidence, rights evidence or authority signals;
- if evidence, date or applicability cannot fit at 270px, the candidate is not production-ready.
